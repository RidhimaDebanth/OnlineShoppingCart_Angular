What is Shopsy?

Shopsy is a reselling platform that allows sellers to sell products online in India. As a seller, you can list on Shopsy, and reach approximately 200-500 million budget-friendly customers. The platform has created a trusted network of distribution between sellers and resellers. As a result, sellers on Shopsy can sell to customers who are not very acquainted with e-shopping via resellers who are people your customers know and trust.
